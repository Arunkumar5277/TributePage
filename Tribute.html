10 Things You didn't know about Albert Einstein

1.Albert Einstein was a brilliant German physicist and scientist.

2. On March 14, 1879, Einstein was born in the German Empire’s Ulm city.

3. He earned a certificate and a PhD from the Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich.

4. In school, his main interests were physics and maths.

5. His first article, released in 1905, established Einstein among the world’s premier scientists.

6. He made significant contributions to physics in 1905 when he discovered the Theory of Special Relativity.

7. He published his ‘General Theory of Relativity’ in 1915.

8. In 1921, he got the Nobel Prize in Physics for his law of photoelectric effect.

9. He died on April 18, 1955, from an Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm.

10. In 1999, he was named ‘Time Person of the Year’ following his demise.
